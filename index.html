About the Game

Pack is a cozy puzzle game where you drag and drop differently shaped items into a suitcase—fit them all before time runs out!

Key Features

  • Intuitive drag‑and‑drop controls
  • Increasingly challenging levels
  • Charming pixel‑art visuals

How to Play

Use your mouse (or touch on mobile) to drag items into the suitcase. Fit shapes perfectly to clear each level!
